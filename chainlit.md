# Welcome to ClaudIA

Your AI trading research assistant powered by Interactive Brokers data.

**What I can do:**
- Check your positions, live orders, and P&L
- Analyze charts — drag a TradingView screenshot into the chat
- Run backtests and generate PineScript strategies
- Scan markets and compute technical indicators
- Propose orders for your review (you confirm, I never act alone)
- Search your past decisions and conversation history

**What I cannot do:**
- Place or modify orders without your explicit confirmation
- Act against your trading principles
